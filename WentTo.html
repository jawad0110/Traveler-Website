Paris, I'm in love! I ticked off the Eiffel Tower, Louvre, and more. The Champs-Élysées was chic, Montmartre was bohemian, and Saint-Germain was elegant. I embraced the Parisian way of life, from croissants to café culture. Paris, you're enchanting! I can't wait to come back!
Paris, France
Switzerland, wow! It was like stepping into a postcard. I mean, the Swiss Alps? They looked like they were straight out of a painting. Lake Geneva was so blue and clear, I could see all the way down to the bottom. Lucerne was like a fairy-tale town, with its cobblestone streets and colorful houses. And Zurich was so clean and modern, with a vibe that was totally unique. Switzerland, you totally blew my mind!
Switzerland
Taj Mahal, India
The Taj Mahal, man! It was incredible. The white marble was glowing, and the carvings were unreal. It felt like I was in a different world. Then I went inside, and the main chamber was even more mind-blowing. It was like a palace fit for a king. The Taj Mahal is seriously one of the best places I've ever been.
